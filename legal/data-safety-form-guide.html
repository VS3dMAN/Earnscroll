Section 2 — Cross-cutting answers

  • Is all of the user data collected by your app encrypted in transit? Yes.
  • Do you provide a way for users to request that their data be deleted? Yes. Settings → Delete Account triggers an Edge Function that deletes all live data and the auth user immediately, plus a 30-day deletion-audit row. Users can also email privacy@earnscroll.com.
  • Did you commit to follow the Play Families Policy? No (target audience is 13+, not the Families program).

Section 3 — Reviewer-trap callouts

Camera. Declare as Used in app, not collected. Be explicit in the rationale: frames are processed on-device by the bundled TFLite MoveNet model and discarded immediately. Cite this in the Permission Declaration video as well.
Health & Fitness. Declare workout counts (squats, pushups, plank seconds) as Collected, Not Shared, Optional, purpose = App functionality + Analytics, encrypted in transit, can be deleted via Delete Account.
Installed apps (QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES). Declare the user's blocker-list selection as Collected, Not Shared, Optional, purpose = App functionality. State explicitly that the FULL device app list is queried only client-side to render the picker, and only the user's selection is persisted on-device.
Accessibility data. Foreground app package name is read on-device only. Do NOT declare it under "Device or other IDs" — it is transient runtime data, never collected or transmitted.

Section 4 — Privacy Policy URL to paste into Play Console

Use the hosted URL once it is live: https://earnscroll.com/privacy. Until then, do not submit for production review — Data Safety form requires a public URL.

Section 5 — Checklists

(a) What to paste into Play Console verbatim

  • Privacy Policy URL: https://earnscroll.com/privacy
  • Per-category answers as above (use the tables in Section 1 — every row is one Play Console question).
  • Permission Declarations for QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES, SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW (if kept), and BIND_ACCESSIBILITY_SERVICE — see legal/play-permission-declarations.md (generated next to this file).

(b) What to keep in sync between Privacy Policy and Data Safety form

  • The list of third-party processors (Supabase, Sentry, Google, Apple).
  • The retention schedule (live: immediate; backups: 45 days; deletion audit: 30 days).
  • The deletion mechanism (Settings → Delete Account + email path).
  • The list of data categories collected. If you add or remove a category in the Privacy Policy, mirror the change here AND in the Settings → Privacy & Data screen.
Owner: the engineer cutting the next Play Store release. Anyone adding a Supabase table, Sentry tag, or new analytics event must update this guide in the same PR.
